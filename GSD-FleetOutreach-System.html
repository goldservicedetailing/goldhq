Setup Guide — Do This Once

Get the System Running in 20 Minutes

1

Add 3 Tracking Columns to Your Sheet

In your fleet tracker spreadsheet, add these column headers after column L (Notes):
M: Sent Date  |  N: Follow-Up 1  |  O: Follow-Up 2

2

Open Apps Script

In your Google Sheet, go to Extensions → Apps Script. Delete any existing code and paste the full script from the Apps Script tab.

3

Update Your Info

At the top of the script, update the GSD config with your name. Everything else (phone, email, website) is already filled in.

4

Run It Once Manually to Test

Click Run → sendInitialOutreach. It will ask for Gmail permissions — click Allow. It will send to the first batch of "To Contact" leads with emails.

5

Set Up Daily Triggers

Go to Triggers (clock icon) → Add Trigger:
• sendInitialOutreach → Time-driven → Daily → 9am
• sendFollowUps → Time-driven → Daily → 10am

6

Watch Your Sheet Update Automatically

Every day the script runs, it will send up to 20 emails, mark them "Contacted," log the date, and follow up on days 3 and 7 — all while you're out detailing cars.

💡 Gmail daily limit: Google allows ~500 emails/day on free Gmail. We cap at 20/day to stay well under the limit and avoid any spam flags. Your 59 leads will be fully contacted within 3 days.
