How to Qualify a Lead Fast
The 5-Question Filter
Q1 · FLEET SIZE
"How many vehicles does your fleet have?" — Need 5+ to be worth a contract
Q2 · CURRENT SOLUTION
"How are you currently handling vehicle cleaning?" — If answer is "we're not" or "it's inconsistent" = hot lead
Q3 · LOCATION
"Are your vehicles based out of one location?" — Need a central yard we can service
Q4 · DECISION MAKER
"Are you the person who handles vendor decisions?" — Get to the right person fast
Q5 · URGENCY
"Is this something you'd want to get started on this month?" — Gauges timing
